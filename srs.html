1.1 Introduction

The AQUAREA Smart Cloud mobile application from Panasonic is an innovative solution for anyone looking to control multiple Panasonic heat pumps in their home. This app is designed to provide users with easy and convenient access to their Panasonic heat pump systems, allowing them to manage and control their home's temperature with just a few taps on their mobile devices.

With the AQUAREA Smart Cloud app, users can control multiple Panasonic heat pumps from anywhere, at any time. This means that you can adjust the temperature, set schedules, and manage other settings for each heat pump in your home remotely, giving you complete control over your home's heating and cooling.

1.2 Purpose

The app is user-friendly, with an intuitive interface that allows users to quickly and easily manage their heat pumps. It provides users with real-time information on the temperature, humidity, and air quality in their home, helping them to make informed decisions about their heating and cooling needs.

1.3 Intended Audience

The intended audience for a mobile application like the one described, which allows users to control multiple heat pumps in their home, is typically homeowners who have installed heat pumps in their residence. These users are looking for a convenient and efficient way to manage the temperature in their home, ensuring that it is always at a comfortable level.
